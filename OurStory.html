Our Story

Regulars Wanted is a beanery which will provide slow food meals at reasonable prices. We look to provide our customers with extraordinary interpretations of familiar classics. By incorporating the sustainable food practices of our ancestors and a staff who’s passion for food and coffee is only surpassed by their dedication to customer service, we look to redefine our patrons expectations for a meal in this genre.
